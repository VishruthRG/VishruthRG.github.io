About Me

Currently a Computer Science major graduate student at Texas A&M University, graduating in May 2023. Prior to graduate school, I completed my Bachelor of Technology from PES University, Bangalore between 2016 to 2020.

Work Experience

My industry experience mainly lies in the microservices, backend scripting and cloud domains.

At Commvault, I am currently in the DevSecOps team working on identifying config drifts in Commvault's SaaS product called Metallic. My main focus is on the security configurations in Azure VMs and on-prem Windows servers.

At Infoblox, I was responsible for developing backend scripts in Go programming language to develop new features for the Infoblox Atlas cloud applications. I have built the services for setting update configurations by containerising Go scripts using docker and putting them up in production in Kubernetes clusters. I have also worked on automated patching of Linux OS using Packer and Vagrant and have written Linux scripts for the same.
